# Privacy Policy for MiloClicker

## Information Collection and Use
MiloClicker does not collect, store, use, or share any personal information or user data.
The app:
- Does not require user accounts
- Does not collect names, email addresses, or identifiers
- Does not access location, contacts, camera, microphone, or storage
- Does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs
- Does not transmit any data over the internet

All interactions occur entirely on the user’s device.

## Third-Party Services
MiloClicker does not use any third-party services that collect or process user data.

## Children’s Privacy
MiloClicker does not knowingly collect any personal information from children or adults. Since no data is collected, there is no risk of personal data exposure.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
If the app’s functionality changes in a way that affects user privacy, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly. Any changes will be reflected in this document.


## Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an Issue.
